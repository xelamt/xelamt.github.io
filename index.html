XELA ART

Hi, I am Alexander Buhagiar Said, a digital artist and student based in Malta, currently studying Digital Arts at the University of Malta.

My work blends bold storytelling, stylised characters, and surreal or symbolic elements to explore themes like identity, transformation, and culture.

From character design to conceptual illustration, I love creating visuals that are both striking and thoughtful. I draw inspiration from pop culture, mythology, music, and everyday life, often with a humorous or unexpected twist.

Currently, I am building my portfolio across digital illustration, animation, and visual storytelling. Whether I am crafting a comic scene, designing an original character, or experimenting with typography, I aim to keep pushing my work in new creative directions.

You can follow my journey or get in touch via @xela.art.mt on Instagram or through the contact page.
